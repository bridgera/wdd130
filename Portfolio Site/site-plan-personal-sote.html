Overview

Purpose

When was the last time you saw an incredible portfolio? Was it plastered in disorganized papers all shoved in a boring beige folder? My guess is that it was probably a beautiful website that caught your eye and sucked you into the artist's work. This is why, as a freelance animator, I am building a site to display my animations, designs, and concept artworks and excite contractors who see it. Every I produce is designed with love and passion, so I want this website to ooze with character and help visitors feel the passion I have for my work.

Audience

My audience for this personal site is those interested in contracting me to produce designs and animations for their creative productions.
